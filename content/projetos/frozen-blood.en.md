I made this game in 9 days for [Game Jaaj 7](https://itch.io/jam/game-jaaj-7), a game jam created by [André Young](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCv29ytb-v_Ph6QZOypGFyEw), and the theme was archenemy.

This was the first Game Jam I ever took part in, and it was quite an experience.

It was 9 days in which I couldn't think of anything else; all the free time I had was spent working on the game.

![](https://img.itch.zone/aW1hZ2UvMTM3NTI4NC84MDI0MDI1LnBuZw==/original/9wBhyG.png)

As it was my first game jam, I suffered from organizational problems, what's expected when you're starting out, the biggest of which was the famous [scope creep](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scope_creep), in my head my game was relatively simple, but for a *solo dev*, in just nine days, it was a bit much, so the enemies had serious collision problems, and the gameplay was very raw.

But apart from that, I'm very proud of this project, especially the visuals. I made all the models used in the game, and I think it turned out really well.

If you want to try out the game, it's available for free on itch.io.
