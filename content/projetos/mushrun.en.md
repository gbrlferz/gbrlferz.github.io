This game started as a silly joke between me and my girlfriend.

I asked her to choose random elements for me to put into a game.

She decided that the game would be about a mushroom that has to collect strawberries in a shopping mall.

![](mushrun/capa.png)

That's when Mu was born, my first original character, a little mushroom with eyeliner and a heart on her head.

I liked the idea so much that I couldn't settle for something simple... I've tried to take this idea in different directions, but I think I'm finally getting there.
