{{% details title="What does _gabiru_ mean?" closed="true" %}}

It's a nickname derived from my real name, Gabriel.

Furthermore, it's a word with several meanings.

- Gabiru is the clever one, the trickster.
- Gabiru is the cheerful one, the joker, the trickster.
- Gabiru is one of the devil's names.
- Gabiru is the one who lives by the game (hehe).

Where I was born, in the northeast of Brazil, that's what they call the black rat.

{{% /details %}}

{{% details title="What do you do?" closed="true" %}}

The areas I have the most practice/interest in are programming, tech art, 3D, level design, game design and writing.

But being a solo developer has made me learn a bit of everything, art, programming, design, UI/UX, music, etc. I have experience in almost everything, no matter how little. I love learning new things, I consider this to be my greatest asset as a professional.

{{% /details %}}


{{% details title="Why do you make games?" closed="true" %}}

I make video games to express myself, I want to bring my ideas into the real world so that others can experience them, and I want to hear what you think about my creations, always evolving. 

My art is _inevitable_, so why not make it my career?
